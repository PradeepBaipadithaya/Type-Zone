Late June, 1863: the fields in Pennsylvania were bountiful with crops, the land  was untouched by conflict, and the people  prospering. Yet a few days later, into this fertile countryside, came a  huge army of Confederate soldiers, led by the ingenious General Robert E. Lee. The great general was leading them to meet up with an even bigger Union  army, known as the Yankees. Howling, shrieking, exploding, tearing, smashing and destroying.. .The  ground was torn up, fences and trees  knocked to splinters, rocks and small  stones were flying in the air... guns were dismounted and the men and horses torn to pieces.
